{About Me}

Hi, y'all! I'm Olivia Ringhiser, a third-year software development student in Cohort 10 at Green River College in Auburn, WA. I had previously received my Associate's Degree from the same school in 2016 through the Running Start Program, and spent three semesters at Washington State University before returning to my roots.

This past summer, I got the chance to be a Software Engineering intern at CDK Global in Seattle. You can even catch a quick glimpse of me in the recruiting video! My hope for this summer is to get an internship through Code 2040, a program in Silicon Valley that partners Black and Latinx technologists with big-name companies in the area. You can check out my résumé here!

After getting my Bachelor's degree, I'd like to spend a few years in industry gaining experience before going back to school to get a Master's Degree in Data Analytics. My dream job is to be a data scientist in the health care field, specializing either in cancer research or women's health.

Outside of class you can usually find me curled up with a good book. I am the second of five children and spend a lot of time with my family. I am a huge dog person; my family has owned five German Shepherds in the past two decades. I love musical theater, sci-fi movies, and travelling (though my student's budget really limits that last one).
